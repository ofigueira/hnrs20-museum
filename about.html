How to Navigate this Museum

If you want to experience the museum in the way the curators have ordered it, you can simply scroll through the landing page and enter each room by clicking on each link in the order displayed until you reach the end. You can also click on the links at the top of the page to jump to a room from any other room, including the landing page. Feel free to spend as much time as you want in any given room, and to go in any order you see fit. We hope you enjoy the experience.
